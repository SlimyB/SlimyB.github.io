Introduction

My name is Ryan, I am currently studying Computer Science with Games Development at the University of Hull. My passion and main goal in life is to make amazing games. Of course that isn't going to come over night, so I currently describe myself as a work in progress.

I am in my final year of my degree course and looking forward to graduating so I can sink my teeth into some projects more personal to me. I have a little blue book that is overrun with ideas and possibilities, but having a degree to concentrate on as well as having a wonderful young daughter to look after, means that spare time is hard to come by.
